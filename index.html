What We Stand For

Reclaim rest. Make it epic.

Out of Office, On Your Terms

We push back, quietly and clearly, against the idea that your worth is your workload. Rest isn’t a reward. It’s maintenance.

Our stance is simple: set the boundary, take the day, live your life. We make the uniform for that stance: clean, durable, signal-strong.

Boundaries First

Say no without theater. Build a line you can keep. Work stays at work; your day off stays yours.

Rest Is Fuel

Sleep, sun, stillness, movement, whatever fills the meter. Rest makes the work better. That’s the point.

Culture > Grind

We choose teams & leaders who protect humans, not just hours. If it costs your health, it’s too expensive.

Signal, Don’t Shout

Minimal design. Big type. Small mark. Wear the message, not the megaphone.
